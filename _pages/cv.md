## Research Experience

### MDTN-GMDA (Jan 2024 - Mar 2026)

Multi-scale Dynamic Temporal Network with Graph Matching Domain Adaptation for cross-subject EEG emotion recognition (Advisor: Prof. Jiahui Pan)

- Designed a multi-scale dynamic temporal attention module combining convolution and multi-head attention to extract robust cross-domain features.
- Proposed a Chebyshev graph-matching domain adaptation framework to align both marginal and conditional distributions.
- Achieved stable improvements on cross-subject SEED and DEAP tasks (SEED single-session accuracy: 94.69%±5.16%).

### PR-DA (Jul 2024 - Jul 2025)

Prototype Regularization Domain Adaptation for cross-subject EEG emotion classification (Advisor: Prof. Jiahui Pan)

- Led the design of prototype regularization and pseudo-label contrastive loss to reduce target-domain representation confusion.
- Implemented adversarial domain alignment for improved cross-domain generalization.
- Reached state-of-the-art performance on SEED, SEED-IV, and SEED-VII benchmarks.
